After the purchase

Installation and commissioning of the EV charging service

All charging stations have to be installed by a professional electrician. However, no special electric car charging skills are required. Stations will connect with the Virta service as soon as they are plugged into the electrical grid.

As a charging station owner, the only thing left to do is make sure that the charging station is commissioned after the installation. If you have been in contact with Virta’s sales representatives, simply follow their instructions. Additional instructions for charging station owners and electricians can also be found on the charging device delivery box.
