Features

Smart features
for public and private charging

Manage your charging stations with ease

All devices sold by Virta are equipped with future-proof smart charging features. Virta’s charging services work on the cloud, which enables us to update new features rapidly. Eliminate the risks of overloading, save money, and charge vehicles securely and safely.

More about features

Payments and billing

Electric car drivers can use charging stations with a mobile application, radio-frequency identification (RFID) tags, or one-time payment solution. All payments are automatically transferred to the charging station owner on a monthly basis.

More about features

Virta Admin Panel

At Virta’s management system, you can monitor your stations, set prices, limit usage, and manage your stations remotely. We will instruct you on how to get started and set a suitable price for your location and customers.

More about features

Smart energy management

Virta's service protects your local grid from peak loads with dynamic load management. You can limit the maximum charging power of stations remotely, or set a maximum charging load for a group of stations.
